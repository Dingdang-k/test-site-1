- Greeney of New York City -

Greenery in New York City is a precious resource. It is a vital component of the urban environment, providing shade, reducing the urban heat island effect, and improving air quality. In this map, I have highlighted the areas in New York City with the highest concentration of green spaces. These areas are essential for the health and well-being of city residents, and they play a crucial role in creating a more sustainable and livable urban environment.
